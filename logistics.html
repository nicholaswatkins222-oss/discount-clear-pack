FOUR PILLARS

How our supply chain works.

Global Sourcing

Our expertise and global connections enable us to source a nearly limitless array of food service packaging, with the ability to customize to your exact specifications. Factory partners across China, Vietnam, and throughout Asia.

Ocean Container Freight

We transport via ocean containers for high-volume shipments — the most cost-efficient route from production to the U.S. We handle vessel booking, container loading, port logistics, and inland transit.

U.S. Warehouses

We ship direct from any of our U.S. warehouses for faster turnaround on stock SKUs and recurring orders. Inventory is positioned to serve regional chains and large-scale enterprises efficiently.

Customs & Duties

Customs, duties, import pricing, and logistical standard operating procedures — all handled internally. Customers see one price, one invoice, one delivery. We manage the complexity behind it.
