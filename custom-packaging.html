CUSTOM PACKAGING

Build your brand
with custom packaging.

Make your food irresistible to shoppers on the go. DCP's options extend shelf-life and have crystal-clear clarity to show off the deliciousness inside.

WHY CUSTOM

An extension of your brand.

DCP is not just another plastic packaging manufacturer. We are creators of packaging together. We are your design experts.

Custom-printed containers are part of your product — they're how shoppers recognize your brand on a shelf, in a deli case, or in a grab-and-go cooler. We treat them with the same seriousness you do.

WHAT YOU CAN CUSTOMIZE

Custom on every dimension.

Our expertise and global connections enable us to source — and customize — a nearly limitless array of food service packaging to your exact specifications.

Custom Printing

Your logo, your colors, your contact info — printed directly on the container. Single-color, multi-color, or full-process printing depending on your spec.

Custom Sizes

Standard SKUs from our catalog, or fully custom dimensions to fit your exact product. We tool to your spec when volumes justify it.

Custom Shapes

Round, rectangular, hex, oval, multi-compartment — if it shows off your product better, we'll source or produce it.

Custom Materials

RPET (recycled PET), PP, PLA, and other food-grade materials. Matched to your application's clarity, rigidity, and sustainability requirements.

Custom Labels & Stickers

When direct printing isn't the right fit, custom labels and seals — produced and applied to your spec.

Custom Packaging Runs

From short pilot runs to high-volume production. We scale with your business — whether that's regional rollout or national distribution.

THE PROCESS

From artwork to first delivery.

01
Discovery
Share your product, brand identity, target shelf placement, and volume estimates. We recommend container, material, and print method.
02
Artwork & Proof
Send your logo and brand assets. We produce a digital proof and a physical sample before any production run.
03
Production
Factory-direct production with our global partners. Quality control at multiple checkpoints before shipment.
04
Delivery
Ocean container freight or direct ship from a U.S. warehouse. Recurring runs managed for consistency at every reorder.
