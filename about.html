WHO WE ARE

Built from the Ground Up in Sparks, Nevada

DCP started with a straightforward observation: food service operators were paying too much for clear packaging from distributors who didn't understand their business. We set out to change that — direct relationships, factory pricing, and containers built for the realities of daily food service operation.

What makes us different isn't just price. It's that we stock across seven product categories so you're not cobbling together orders from three vendors. It's that every SKU in our catalog can carry your logo. It's that we've been doing this for over a decade and we're not going anywhere.

Today, DCP ships nationwide to sushi restaurants, delis, bakeries, dispensaries, and distributors of all sizes. Our clients range from single-location operators to regional chains. The common thread: they all need reliable containers, on time, at a price that works.

SUSTAINABILITY

Clear on Our Responsibility

Clear packaging and environmental responsibility aren't opposites. Here's how DCP approaches sustainability.

Recyclable Materials

The majority of our clear containers are made from RPET (recycled PET) — one of the most widely accepted recyclable plastics in the US. Containers can re-enter the materials stream after use.

Food-Safe Standards

Every product in our catalog meets FDA food-contact material standards. No compromise on food safety — ever. The same material transparency we offer in our containers, we apply to sourcing.

Responsible Sourcing

We work with suppliers who meet quality and compliance standards at the production level — not just at the label. Our sourcing process is intentional, not just transactional.
